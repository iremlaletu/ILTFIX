Frequently Asked Questions

Do The Repairs Come With A Warranty?

All repairs done in store, or through ILT Tech or Mail In repair services are backed by our 6 months warranty. Our warranty protects against Screen/LCD and any other repaired or replaced parts that malfunction, or do not work as intended or designed. It does not protect against any of the following: Accidental or purposeful drops; for example breaking/cracking LCD or Digitizer glass. Tampering with internal hardware. Damage resulting from attempted customer repair. Unlocked or jail broken devices. New damages unrelated to the original repair. Or Water damage.

What type of part are going to be used in my phone and where do they come from?

The parts used in your repairs typically come from various sources: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) Parts: Sourced directly from the phone manufacturer, ensuring high quality and compatibility. Aftermarket Parts: Third-party alternatives to OEM parts, usually cheaper but varying in quality. Refurbished or Used Parts: Salvaged from recycled or damaged phones, offering a more affordable option. Generic or Generic-Compatible Parts: Generic components claiming compatibility, but quality may vary.

What happens if you find other problems with my device or cause more damage to my phone during the repair?

If we discover additional issues or inadvertently cause further damage during the repair process, we take full responsibility for resolving the issue promptly and professionally at no extra cost to you.
